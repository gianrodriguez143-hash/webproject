What to prepare

Requirements by level

Nursery – Kindergarten

  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)

Grade 1

  • ECCD Checklist (original copy)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)

Grade 2 – Grade 10

  • Report Card (Form 138A)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character

Grade 11 – Grade 12

  • Report Card (F–138A)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Certificate of Completion in Junior High
  • ESC Certificate (private school completer)

Freshmen (College)

  • 4 pcs 1x1 ID picture
  • Drug test result
  • Report Card (F–138)
  • Medical certificate
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Marriage contract (if married)

Transferee (College)

  • TOR (evaluative copy)
  • Honorable dismissal
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Medical certificate
  • Drug test result
  • 4 pcs 1x1 ID picture
  • Marriage contract (if married)

Diploma Program

  • Report Card (F–138) — High School / Senior High
  • Transcript of Records (College)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (1 photocopy)
  • Indigent Certificate (from Barangay)
  • Proof of Residency (DSWD)
  • VAX card (photocopy)

Enrollment Procedures

Choose the track that matches your student to see the exact steps.

See the Guidance-in-charge for the entrance examination / interview.

Secure the Eligibility Form from the Principal's Office.

Present the PSA Birth Certificate and Eligibility Form to the Registrar's Office, then secure and accomplish the Registration Form.

Proceed to the Finance Office for tuition levelling.

Pay the enrollment fee at the cashier.

Go back to the Registrar's Office to complete enrollment.
