Getting There

How to Reach Bislig

01

✈️ By Air

Fly to Butuan City Airport (BXU) via Cebu Pacific or PAL from Manila or Cebu. Travel time from Butuan to Bislig is approximately 4–5 hours by bus or van.

Manila → Butuan → Bislig
02

🚌 By Bus

Several bus lines operate routes from Davao City to Bislig, with travel times of approximately 5–6 hours. From Surigao City, take a bus south through Tandag.

Davao → Bislig (5–6 hrs)
03

🚐 Local Transport

Tricycles, habal-habal (motorcycle taxis), and vans-for-hire are the main ways to reach individual spots from Bislig City Proper. Full-day tours run ₱800–1,200.

City Proper → Spots · ₱800–1,200
